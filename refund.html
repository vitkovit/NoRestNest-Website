Refunds

Last updated: 2026-06-21

1. Who processes refunds

All NoRestNest subscriptions and purchases are sold through Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). Apple and Google are the merchants of record. They process all refunds — we don't.

2. EU 14-day right of withdrawal

Under EU Consumer Rights Directive 2011/83/EU you normally have 14 days to withdraw from a digital-content purchase. When you confirm a purchase in the App Store or Google Play and start using Pro features, you expressly consent to immediate execution of the contract and acknowledge that you lose this right (Art. 16(m) CRD). If you have not yet started using Pro features after purchase, you can still request a refund through Apple or Google within 14 days.

3. How to request a refund

App Store (iOS): visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in, find your NoRestNest purchase, and select Request a refund.

Google Play (Android): open Play Store → profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → NoRestNest → Request a refund.

Apple and Google decide. We have no override.

4. Cancelling is not a refund

Cancelling a subscription stops the next charge. You keep Pro access until the current billing period ends. Cancelled subscriptions are not refunded for the remaining period.

5. Questions

For questions about this policy, contact contact@norestnest.com. For privacy or data requests, contact privacy@norestnest.com.
