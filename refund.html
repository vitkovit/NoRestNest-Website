Refund Policy for NoRestNest

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

At NoRestNest, we want you to be satisfied with your purchase. This Refund Policy explains how refunds work for our subscription plans and one-time purchases.

📱 Important: Refunds are Processed by App Stores

NoRestNest does not process refunds directly. All purchases are handled by Google Play Store (Android) or Apple App Store (iOS). Refund requests must be submitted through your respective app store.

1. Refund Eligibility

Subscription Type Refund Window Eligibility
60-Day Free Trial Anytime during trial Cancel anytime at no cost (no refund needed)
Pro Monthly ($4.99/month) Within 48 hours of purchase Eligible for refund via Google Play or App Store
Pro Yearly ($29.99/year) Within 48 hours of purchase Eligible for refund via Google Play or App Store
Pro Lifetime ($79.99 one-time) Within 48 hours of purchase Eligible for refund via Google Play or App Store

2. How to Request a Refund

For Android (Google Play Store)

  1. Open the Google Play Store app on your device
  2. Tap your profile icon in the top right
  3. Go to Payments & Subscriptions → Subscriptions
  4. Select NoRestNest
  5. Tap Request a Refund
  6. Follow the on-screen instructions to submit your request

Alternatively, you can:

  • Visit Google Play Subscriptions on the web
  • Contact Google Play Support directly for assistance

For iOS (Apple App Store)

  1. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find your NoRestNest purchase
  4. Click Report a Problem
  5. Select Request a Refund and follow the prompts

Alternatively:

  • Open the App Store app
  • Tap your profile icon
  • Go to Purchased → Find NoRestNest
  • Tap Report a Problem

3. Refund Processing Time

  • Google Play: Refunds are typically processed within 3-5 business days after approval
  • Apple App Store: Refunds are typically processed within 48 hours to 5 business days after approval
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the purchase
  • Bank processing times may add an additional 1-3 business days

4. Canceling Your Subscription (No Refund Needed)

If you want to cancel your subscription to avoid future charges but do not need a refund for the current period:

Google Play (Android)

  1. Open Google Play Store
  2. Go to Subscriptions
  3. Select NoRestNest
  4. Tap Cancel Subscription
  5. Confirm cancellation

Apple App Store (iOS)

  1. Open Settings on your device
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select NoRestNest
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

Note: When you cancel a subscription, you retain access to Pro features until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account reverts to the free tier.

5. What Happens After a Refund

  • Your subscription will be immediately canceled
  • Access to Pro features (cloud sync, unlimited programs, advanced analytics) will be revoked
  • Your workout data and progress photos will remain accessible in the free tier (local device only)
  • Cloud-synced data will no longer sync across devices without an active Pro subscription

6. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds are generally NOT available in the following cases:

  • Requests submitted more than 48 hours after purchase (Google Play policy)
  • Pro-rated refunds for unused portions of a subscription (not supported by app stores)
  • Refunds for auto-renewals that you forgot to cancel (unless within 48 hours of renewal)
  • Change of mind after using Pro features extensively
  • Violation of our Terms of Service

⚠️ Important: Google Play and Apple have the final authority on all refund requests. NoRestNest does not control or override their refund decisions.

7. Special Circumstances

If you believe you qualify for a refund but your request was denied by Google Play or Apple, or if you have a unique situation, please contact us:

  • Email: support@norestnest.com
  • Subject: "Refund Request - [Your Order ID]"
  • Include: Purchase receipt, order ID, reason for refund request

While we cannot override app store decisions, we will do our best to assist you and provide guidance.

8. Free Trial Policy

  • NoRestNest offers a 60-day free trial to all new users
  • No credit card required to start the trial
  • You can cancel anytime during the trial at no cost
  • After 60 days, you must purchase a Pro subscription to continue using Pro features
  • The free trial is available once per user/device

9. Billing Disputes

If you notice unexpected charges or billing issues:

  1. Check your Google Play or App Store purchase history to confirm the charge
  2. Verify that auto-renewal wasn't enabled on your subscription
  3. Contact Google Play Support or Apple Support directly
  4. If the issue persists, email us at support@norestnest.com with your order details

10. Changes to This Refund Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time to reflect changes in app store policies or our services. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification or email

Contact Us

If you have questions about our refund policy or need assistance with a refund request, please contact us:

  • Email: support@norestnest.com
  • Support Hours: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM EST
  • Response Time: Within 24-48 hours
