What it does

Everything you need at the rack.

No social feed, no ads, no fluff. Open it, log your set, get back to lifting.

Progressive overload, precalculated

Easy set? +2.5% next time. Hard? Hold. Failed? Back off 10%. The app does the math, rounds to your plate increment, and pre-fills every set — per set number, with double progression when you cap the reps. Not vibes. Not AI. Just arithmetic.

Log a set in two taps

Pre-filled weight and reps. Tap a feel (easy / good / hard / failed). Rest timer starts itself. Reorder exercises mid-workout without losing anything.

Three modes, one tracker

Weight + reps for the barbell stuff. Reps only for push-ups, pull-ups, dips. Time for planks and dead hangs. Each one progresses on its own profile — Standard, Conservative, Aggressive, or your own custom percentages.

Hit your macros without doing math

Scan a barcode, snap your plate, or search the food database. Calories, protein, carbs, fat — logged. Targets show what you've still got to eat today.

See every muscle you trained

Tap a muscle on the body view. See the last session that hit it, what's recovered, what's overdue. Stops the "wait, did I do shoulders this week?" guessing game.

Watch the numbers climb

PRs on every lift. Weekly volume per muscle group. Bodyweight trend. One screen. Pulled from real sessions — no estimates, no projections, no marketing.

What it isn't

Things NoRestNest won't do.

Because there are already too many apps doing them.

It won't time how long you rest.

You're an adult. Rest as long as you need. There's a timer if you want one. Nobody's watching the clock for you.

It won't break your streak when life happens.

Missed Tuesday because your kid got sick? Cool. There's no streak. There's no shame banner. You just lift when you're back.

It won't pretend to be your coach.

The math suggests the next weight. That's it. No motivational quote, no fake voice in your head, no "you got this!" notification at 6am.

It won't gamify your gains.

No XP. No levels. No badges for "Squat Apprentice — Tier 2." You're not collecting points. You're collecting muscle.

It won't ping you 14 times a day.

Zero push notifications by default. Your phone has enough screaming at you.

It won't sell your data. To anyone.

No ad SDKs, no analytics broker, no "anonymized aggregate" cop-out. Your protein intake is not a product.
